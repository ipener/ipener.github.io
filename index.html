Yes, it’s a supercomputer

disguised as a beautiful floor that performs useful computations and simultaneously heats your home. And because it's a computer, it automatically adjusts the temperature—down to every 50cm and almost instantly.

notificationsLearn about our API

It heats during a power outage

and provides backup energy thanks to integrated batteries2—ideal to use alongside solar panels. The precisely engineered tiles are durable, waterproof and easily carry any furniture or even cars.

And it scales to virtually any size

since it's composed of modular, easily replaceable tiles3. Apartment, house or office—Furoa’s slick light or deep gray ceramic surface will fit any interior.

The specs

  • Width × Depth × Height: 1000 × 1000 × 15mm
  • Weight: 9.9kg
  • Power consumption (sleep mode): ~1W
  • Peak power consumption: 60–100W
  • Battery capacity: 200Wh
  • Surface temperature: Up to 35° C
  • Operating temperature: -25° C to -65° C
  • Relative humidity: 5% to 85%
  • Line voltage: 100–125V AC at 12A; 220–240V AC at 6A
  • Frequency: 50Hz to 60Hz, single phase
  • Maximum load: TBD
  • Maximum altitude: TBD
